I am visiting Jesse in Santander; we did a nice chill ride on his mountain bikes out to Isla Virgen del Mar, riding along some small roads and paths near the coastline. At Virgen del Mar, we sat and had a coffee and did some wandering to look at the waves. There was a swell happening and so the waves were big, crashing into the cliffs in big explosions and sprays. I stopped here and there to take some photos along the ride. The coastline in the area is a kind of protected nature zone, blocked from full-scale urbanization, yet also used in various historically-inherited and flexibly-governed ways.
