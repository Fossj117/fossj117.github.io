After taking a day off following our longer ride to the farm near Santa Pau, we did a shorter day of local exploration by bike. We first rode out to a waterfall area nearby which turned out to be quite pretty, and without many other folks around. Perhaps due to my routing choice, the ride down included a chunkier section of trail which was fun and still manageable on our rented gravel bikes. After the waterfall, we rode uphill a long while back through Santa Pau towards Olot to stop at a restaurant that A liked. I had one of the Santa Pau bean dishes (Fesols de Santa Pau), which I like a lot. The bean preparations around there are something I'd like to try at home maybe -- I like the texture they go for (less "soupy" than a lot of bean dishes I have), and the combo with sausage. There was a nice view of the Pirenees (I think) on the ride toward Olot.
