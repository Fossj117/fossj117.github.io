Current stovetop popcorn best practices (March 2026): 

1. I like a large or medium sized white/yellow kernel. 
2. I pop in grapeseed oil in the whirley pop.
3. Put stove to fairly high heat (7-8), and pop a few kernels.
4. When ready, usually add about 1/3rd cup of kernels for a full batch. 
5. Turn handle while popping; when slows down, take off heat and open lid to vent steam.
6. For toppings, need a bowl with a lid. 
7. Add 1-3 tablespoons of butter, melted. 
8. Add salt (finely ground in my grinder) and similarly ground black pepper (right now: tellicherry peppercorns)
9. Put lid on bowl and shake; sometimes do this in two passes to get good coverage.
