Jeff's guidelines for walking in the city: 

1. Walk at a leisurely pace and look at things. 
2. Stop to inspect things when you are curious. Notice what is different from last time. 
3. Wear good shoes that won't hurt your feet after a while. 
4. Bring the big backpack because you might need it for something.
5. Don't wear headphones. Don't be on your phone.
6. Say hi to people when you walk by. "Good morning" or "Hello".
7. Bring camera to take photos when inspired to do so.  
8. Look at what is in the little library boxes. 
9. Think about what it might be good to bring before you go out.
