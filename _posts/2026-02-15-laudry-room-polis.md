The apartment laundry room raises many questions: 

* The person before me has left their laundry in the washer even though the cycle is over. How long do I have to wait before I move their laundry out to use the machine? 

* Is it okay to use multiple washers or dryers at the same time? 

* Can I claim "next" on the laundry machine? How do I do that? 

* What should I do if I find a lost piece of clothing? 

Who gets to decide the answers to these questions, and how?
