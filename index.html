Previously, I was a member of the data science team at Airbnb, received my AM in Statistics at Harvard University, my BA in Mathematics and Philosophy from Williams College, and studied at Oxford University. I have also been affiliated with the Berkman Klein Center for Internet and Society both as research intern and as an Assembly Student Fellow. You can find a copy of my CV here.

In addition to reseach, I enjoy creative projects, especially at the intersection of technology & visual art. See the posts section of this site for (mostly) informal writing & experimentation on a range of topics.
